IVI Foundation Assistance

The IVI Foundation provides additional assistance through the IVI Web Forum and  IVI Learning Resources.

IVI Web Forum

The IVI Web Forum is an open, moderated IVI Web Forum for anything related to IVI. Topics for discussion include:

  • Use and development of IVI drivers
  • SCPI
  • VISA
  • VXI Plug & Play

The forum is monitored by active members of the IVI Foundation itself, including many of the chief architects of the IVI specifications. It is an excellent resource for understanding many of the general issues one encounters when using IVI drivers in various application development environments (ADEs).

Note:

While the web forum can be helpful for general IVI issues, questions specific to the KtNA driver or to the instrument itself should be directed to Keysight Technologies.

Other IVI Learning Resources

The IVI Foundation also maintains several other resources for learning about IVI driver usage:

  • IVI Getting Started Guide provides short introductions to using IVI drivers in specific ADEs
  • IVI Getting Started Videos provide guided tutorials on using IVI drivers in various ADEs
  • Understanding the Benefits of IVI (white paper) provides an overview of many of the main motivations for using IVI drivers to build test systems
  • IVI Driver Fundamentals Course is a two-hour instructor-led session open to the public and offered free of charge at IVI Foundation meetings in the U.S., Europe, and China

Access these resources at IVI Learning Resources.
