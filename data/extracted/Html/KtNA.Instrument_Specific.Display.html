Attributes

Name Description
ActiveTraceIsolationEnabled Maximizes (isolates) or restores the active trace in the active window.
Enabled Specifies whether to disable or enable all analyzer display information in all windows in the analyzer application.
ErrorMessageEnabled Enables and disables error pop-up messages on the display.
FrequencyAnnotationEnabled Turns frequency information on the display title bar ON or OFF for all windows.

SpinResolution Sets and returns the resolution of the front panel knob when it is used to adjust Source Power manually.
StatusBarEnabled Turns the status bar at the bottom of the screen ON or OFF. The status bar displays information for the active window.
UpdatesEnabled Enables or disables display updates.
VNAAppEnabled Makes the VNA application visible or not visible.
