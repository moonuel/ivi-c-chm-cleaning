Attributes

Name Description
Delay Specifies the delay in seconds that should be applied by the VNA after the Aux trigger output is output and before the acquisition is made.
Duration Specifies the width of the output pulse, which is the time that the Aux trigger output will be asserted.
Mode Specifies how often a trigger output signal is sent.
Polarity Specifies the polarity of the Aux Output signal being supplied by the VNA.
Position Specifies whether the aux trigger out signal is sent BEFore or AFTer the acquisition.
